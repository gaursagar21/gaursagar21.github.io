About

I'm Sagar — a backend engineer who likes building things that scale and occasionally break in interesting ways.

Currently at Ema, working on AI infrastructure. Before that, I was the founding backend engineer at Markov ML, where I built an agentic AI platform from scratch — the kind of zero-to-one work where you're writing the architecture doc and the code in the same week.

I spent my earlier years at MindTickle, joining during Series B and sticking around through their unicorn run. Mostly I was the person called when something needed to handle 100x more traffic by next quarter.
