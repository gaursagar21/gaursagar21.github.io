About

I'm Sagar — a backend engineer focused on building scalable, reliable systems.

Currently at Ema on the Customer Experience (CX) team, building the systems and infrastructure that keep AI agents running in production. Most of my work is around scalability and reliability: high-volume data ingestion, handling (and preventing) messy failure modes, and making a non-deterministic system shippable. I’ve also made a few minor contributions on the voice side. Before that, I was the first backend engineer at Markov ML, where I built a no-code AI platform from the ground up.

I spent my earlier years at MindTickle, joining right after they raised their Series B and leaving after they'd hit unicorn status. I worked on backend systems, scaling, and reliability as the product (and traffic) grew.
